The Validator is a tool to scan through thousands of files and to find errors in them.

TO USE (brief instructions)
1. Open Validator.exe
2. Fill in the "Game Path" field at the top, and choose the appropriate game from the list on the right.
3. If you are using moddir, fill in the "Mod Name" field.
4. Click on "Validate", at the top right, and wait for validation to run.
